# 🏢 AreaIntel AI

**AreaIntel AI is an advanced deep research agent designed for intelligent real estate market analysis and data-driven property insights.**

AreaIntel AI produces detailed, factual research reports with full citations by orchestrating specialized 'planner' and 'execution' agents. Built on a high-quality system architecture, it addresses common research hurdles like misinformation, speed, and token limitations by utilizing parallelized scraping and recursive deep research workflows.

**Our mission is to empower professionals with accurate, high-quality, and actionable intelligence through stable and scalable AI architecture.**

## 🌟 Why AreaIntel AI?

- **Market Precision:** Specialized logic for identifying real estate trends and neighborhood growth metrics.
- **Current Data:** Scrapes live web data to ensure reports aren't based on outdated training sets.
- **Long-Form Reporting:** Bypasses token limits to generate comprehensive research exceeding 2,000 words.
- **Unbiased Aggregation:** Scrapes 20+ sources per query to minimize bias and cross-reference facts.

## 🏗️ Architecture

The system utilizes a sophisticated multi-agent flow to ensure stability and depth:
1. **Planner Agent:** Breaks down a complex query into specific research sub-questions.
2. **Execution Agents:** Parallelized crawlers that gather information from verified web sources and local documents.
3. **Publisher Agent:** Aggregates findings, filters for relevance, and compiles the final professional report.
